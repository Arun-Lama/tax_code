Our Story

Born on Eating Street

Holy Grill started with a simple belief: a great burger doesn't need a hundred options — it needs five perfect ones. We set up shop on Eating Street in the heart of Purple Haze, Thamel, where locals and travelers hunt for something real.

We studied the legends — the smash technique of Shake Shack, the loaded builds of Five Guys, the simplicity of In-N-Out — then added our own Kathmandu fire. Every patty is hand-formed, every sauce made in-house, every bun baked fresh.
